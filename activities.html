Adventure Tour


        ActivitesAre you a die-hard fan of adventure and seeking some adventurous activities around India? Manali is the first choice to launch yourself in different kinds of adventure. From paragliding to skiing, mountaineering to bungee jumping, trekking to water sports, Manali is every traveler's need of the hour. Get your inflated boat row through the frothy cold river and be a part of the thrilling experience with river rafting at Beas. Leaving the splashes of water behind, try touching heights with paragliding in Solang Valley and let your heart fill with excitement and a bit of fear while flying amidst the blue skies of Manali. In case you wish to explore the trekking trails of Himachal, get your socks pulled up and head towards Bhrigu Lake for a magical experience. Follow the trekking trails amidst the snowy peaks, gravelly cliff sides, and alpine meadows to witness some great panoramic views down the mountain. Enjoy every bit of being in Himachal, the state of adventure and experience the lifetime joy of skiing down the slopes, mountaineering mountains with high altitudes, camping under the stars and all that takes you to the roads of the venture.
